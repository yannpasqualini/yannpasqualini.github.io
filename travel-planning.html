FAQ

Quick answers before you start.

Is this a guided hike?

No. The Travel Planner is an advisory service for self-guided travel. For a guided day out, please see the Hikes page.

I don’t want hiking.

No problem. We can build a trip focused on heritage, villages, scenic drives, museums, churches, gentle nature, etc.

How fast will I receive my PDF?

Goal: 48h after receiving a complete questionnaire. In high season, it may vary slightly depending on workload (I’ll tell you clearly).

I’m travelling as a family / couple.

Very common. We adapt pace, driving times and breaks, and avoid unrealistic days.

Can you plan the GR20?

Yes, as advice only (route, stages, season, logistics, variants). I do not provide GPX tracks.

I’m not sure which package to choose.

If you’re unsure, Premium (with a call) is often the most comfortable. Otherwise, message me and I’ll advise you quickly.
