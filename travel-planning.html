In short

I’m Yann Pasqualini, based in Biguglia (near Bastia), Corsica. I work on the ground as a mountain professional and design bespoke Corsica itineraries.

Distances on a map can be misleading here. Mountain roads, seasonality and terrain conditions matter. As a Corsica Travel Planner, I build realistic, structured travel plans adapted to your pace and expectations.

The goal is not to overload your days, but to create a coherent, fluid experience you can follow independently.
