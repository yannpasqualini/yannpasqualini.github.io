FAQ

Quick answers before you start.

Is this a guided tour?

No. This is a planning advisory service for an independent (self-guided) trip. If you want a guided hike, please check the “Hikes” page.

I don’t want hiking.

No problem. We can build a trip focused on heritage, villages, scenic drives, beaches, museums, easy nature walks, etc.

How long to receive my PDF?

Target: 48 hours after I receive your full questionnaire. In high season, it can vary slightly depending on workload — I’ll always be transparent.

We travel as a couple / family.

Very common. I adapt pace, driving times, breaks and day structure so the trip feels smooth (and not exhausting).

Can you help for the GR20?

Yes — as planning advice (stages, season, logistics, options). I don’t provide GPX tracks and I don’t replace mountain judgement on the ground.

I don’t know which level to choose.

If you hesitate, Premium (with the video call) is often the most comfortable. You can also message me and I’ll point you to the best option.
